Answer: Foxes neither want pretty flowers nor storybooks. Hen houses aren't in forests. The fox walked from the city into the forest looking for its natural habitat since forests are one of the main natural habitats of foxes. The answer is {C}.

Question: When a person has to make large quantities of food at once, what kitchen device do they likely use? Answer Choices: (a) microwave (b) blender (c) toaster (d) oven (e) mixer
